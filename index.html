The framework
Let implied metrics (IV, put/call, skew, dispersion, gamma) size your tail-risk expectations — but let the realized tape (vol level, big-day frequency, drawdown depth, drift smoothness) decide risk-on vs. derisk. By that standard, February said risk-on; today says derisk — and the realized trigger fired for the first time in 2026 on June 5.
Honesty caveats: the 60% threshold was chosen after seeing the data (in-sample) — trust the dimension, not the exact number. This is n=1 on the phase change. The rule accepted March's −12% dip as the price of staying in trends. Not investment advice.
